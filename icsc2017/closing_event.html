ICSC2017

Closing event

Sunday Oct. 1, 20:30

Salón Quitapenas

Paysandú 847 ➜

The ICSC2017 will close on Sunday evening with a meeting at the old building where the legendary Bar Quitapenas used to be. The live performance of the collective composition "Quitapenas 4/4" will be followed by a delicious asado.

Program

Quitapenas 4/4

for eight marimbones and 16 audio channels

Collective composition by Marvin Brendel, Guzman Calzada, Lukas Kühne and Ulrich Schrader
Concept: Lukas Kühne

Asado and tapas

Traditional Uruguayan barbecue with a table of vegan tapas
